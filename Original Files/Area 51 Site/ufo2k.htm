UFO2000 Terrains

To install these terrains simply unzip the file to the newmaps folder of UFO2000 and you're all set to play the terrain, either on Hot Seat or against other players (make sure both have the same version).

The terrains are organized by the game they require to be installed previously in UFO2000. You can also download a Terrain pack containing all of these terrains (Area 51, Apartment, Farm with UFOs, Modified Farm, Native, Polis, Railyard, Siberia, Skyranger Hangar, Slum).

UFO (Full version)

Mad City  (V.???) – Variation of the original City. Contains its buildings but facing different directions.

Polis (v. 9.02) – 6 large city maps (60x60) plus random generated terrain for 5x5 and 4x4 map sizes.

Native (v. 9.03) – Combat in a tropical village. Lots of unconventional buildings, cultivated areas, gardens and palm trees.

Siberia (v. 9.02) – Combat in snow filled canyons and an underground base.

Modified Farm (v. 9.03) – Variation of the Farm terrain, including some new maps.

UFO (Demo Version)

Area 51 (v. 9.52)– Before installing this please delete any previous version (the folders and .lua files). It only consists now of a single terrain (instead of the previous 5). Later I shall release Assault and probably some of the larger maps as separate terrains. Edit: It now includes the Skyranger Hangar, a remake of the previous Skyranger Factory.

Farm with UFOs – A classic.

Apartment (v. 9.04)– The UFO2000 version of the City mod. Lots of 3-level buildings, including a movie theatre.

TFTD (Full version)

Railyard (v. 9.02)– Train lines, tunnels and buildings.

Slum – Poor quality housing, ruined buildings, a 3 level highway and lots of trash (literally).
